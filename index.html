About Me

Lisbon, Portugal

Hiya! My name is Adi. I am a business oriented product designer and digital storyteller. My past experience has been primarily in 0-1 projects and more recently with AI/ML systems.

I am a thoughtful and collaborative designer working at the intersection of user needs and business objectives. I believe in using human centered design methodologies to define product strategy and design product interfaces/experiences to drive business growth. My endeavour in design is a balancing act between the holistic view of a product and delving into tiny details of an interface.

In the past I have gotten positive feedback for my skills in product thinking and interaction design.
